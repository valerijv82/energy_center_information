The Enaktiva Energy Centrals delivers renewable thermal energy to buildings in Norway
heating, hot water and cooling.

The different centrals are established to ensore to the developer's desire to ensure an energy solution that takes into account sustainability, stability and costs in the best possible way, and which does not place operation and maintenance tasks on consumers who have neither the time nor the expertise to do so. The Energy Centrals have solid owners who use professional suppliers to operate the plant in a proper way, so that each individual customer does not have to worry about the heat delivery



Myrvoll Energi AS supplies heating and hot water for the area around Myrvoll Station. The plant collects renewable heat from the ground and the drain, and uses heat pumps that raise the temperature for heating purposes. As a reserve on extra cold days or case of maintenance, the plant has an electric boiler that uses electricity.



Billingstad Energi AS supplies heating and hot water for the area around Vestre Billingstad. The plant collects renewable heat from the ground through over 100 energy wells, and uses heat pumps that raise the temperature for heating purposes. As a reserve on extra cold days or case of maintenance, the plant has an electric boiler that uses electricity.
